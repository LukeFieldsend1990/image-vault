About Me
"don't believe the hype" · "this is a low" · "i'd rather be a forester than sit in a maths class" (not a real quote i made it up)

alright. it's Luke. 16, from Belfast, you probably know me off the bus or from gigs in the Limelight / Spring & Airbrake / the wee all-ages shows at Auntie Annie's. da's a sparky, ma works in an office, we're not loaded but there's always a guitar in the house so that'll do me.

i basically play guitar every spare hour — got a battered Squier Strat i'm saving to replace, a Boss DS-1 and a practice amp i've gaffer-taped back together about nine times. me and a couple of lads have a band (name changes every fortnight, currently "The Tannoy", don't laugh). we've done exactly two gigs and one of them was a youth club.

other thing i'm into: messing with computers. i taught myself HTML off the view-source button and a Neopets guide, then did this whole profile myself — the CSS, the layout, the lot (go on, view source). built the band a website on a free Geocities/Freewebs thing with a hit counter and everything. half tempted to do this for a living. apparently you can study it at Queen's. we'll see where i am in 10 yrs — bet it's something daft like running engineering teams in London ha, as if.

don't: add me then send nothing. i'm not a number.
do: tell me a band i've not heard. tab me something good.
